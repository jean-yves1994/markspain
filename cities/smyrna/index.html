Smyrna Team


Are you looking to buy or sell a home in Smyrna? With over 350 homes for sale, this 15 square-mile suburb has plenty to offer those looking to buy a house and plenty of competition for those looking to sell. Smyrna, Georgia is located in the northwest of the metropolitan ring, and sits at the junction of I-75 and I-285, which provides easy access to just about anywhere in Atlanta. Smyrna has a continuously growing population, currently sitting around 56,000.

Whether you’re looking to buy or sell your house in Smyrna, you’ll most likely have plenty of options, as there are over 25,000 households. Smyrna continues to grow in popularity due to its many attractions, as well as the fact that it neighbors several desirable and happening spots, including Vinings, Dunwoody, Marietta, and Roswell. Downtown Smyrna is home to Village Green, a gorgeous town center for government buildings, the library, markets, and more.

Smyrna Team

The Smyrna Team remains competitive as more and more people are moving to the Atlanta area. With the addition of Truist Park and the Battery Atlanta, Smyrna recently became the host of the Atlanta Braves, which attracts fans, tourists, and locals even on non-game days. This has caused a spike in both Marietta and Smyrna real estate demand. Smyrna, Georgia has a median household income of around $76,000 and the average listing price of a property in Smyrna hovers around $395,000.

Buy a Home In Smyrna

If you’re looking to buy a home in Smyrna, be prepared to enter a decently competitive real estate market. Many people find the Smyrna area appealing, as it boasts several major employers and small businesses, as well as more affordable housing than either downtown living or the nearby wealthy suburbs. Additionally, Smyrna has plenty of things to do for everyone, such as enjoyable nightlife and entertainment, parks, breweries, golf courses, and more. Smyrna’s most notable employers include:

  • UCB
  • Atlanta Bread Company
  • Eaton Corporation
  • IBM
  • Emory–Adventist Hospital at Smyrna
