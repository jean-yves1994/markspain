Dunwoody Team


Are you looking to buy or sell a home in Dunwoody? See our Dunwoody listings below! Dunwoody is a city located just ten miles north of Atlanta, comfortably situated in DeKalb County. Although it was established as a city as recently as 2008, its history dates back to the 1830s. Dunwoody has six eclectic neighborhoods and is home to 49,371 residents. Dunwoody is home to malls, businesses, restaurants, and many amenities residents and visitors love.

With proximity to I-285, Dunwoody offers its residents easy commute times and access to key locations in Atlanta, Sandy Springs, Roswell, and Alpharetta. Currently, the average listing price for a home in Dunwoody is $490,000, and the average household income is $95,057.
