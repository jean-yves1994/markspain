Are you looking to buy or sell a home in Hapeville, GA? See our Hapeville listings below! A busy city in the south of Fulton County, Hapeville is home to almost 6,600 people. This 2.41 square mile community was incorporated into Fulton County in 1853 and has enjoyed prosperous growth ever since its establishment. With a wide variety of hotels, restaurants, attractions, schools, and opportunities, many homebuyers are attracted to Hapeville real estate.

Neighboring East Point, College Park, and Riverdale, Hapeville is conveniently located near key communities in the Atlanta metropolitan area. One of the highlights of Hapeville is its proximity to the Atlanta Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport. The average sale price for a house in Hapeville is $287,500, and the average household income is $44,881.
