Chamblee Team


Are you looking to buy or sell a home in Chamblee? See our Chamblee listings below! Located in northern Dekalb County, Chamblee is home to 29,232 residents. With a history dating back to the late nineteenth century, Chamblee is a community that keeps on growing into a prosperous suburban center near Atlanta. This 3.1 square-mile community is among the highlights of Dekalb County due to its many activities, parks, and restaurants ideal for families and singles alike. 

Neighboring popular suburban centers such as Brookhaven, Dunwoody, Doraville, and Tucker. Chamblee offers residents and visitors smooth commutes and easy access to key areas near the Atlanta metropolitan area. Currently, the median household income in Chamblee is $61,797, while the median listing price for a house for sale in Chamblee is $400,000.
