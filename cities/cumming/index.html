Cumming Team


Looking to buy or sell a house in Cumming, GA? See our Cumming listings below! Located in Forsyth County, this exurban city is home to 6,309 people. Founded by Cherokee Natives in the mid 18th century, Cumming has faced substantial growth in recent history. Now a commuter town, Cumming has gone far from its humble, rural roots. Homeowners and visitors alike enjoy many of Cumming’s diverse amenities, business opportunities, hiking trails, and year-round fishing and boating.

Located north of Atlanta, Cumming neighbors Suwanee, Alpharetta, and Roswell. Cumming offers residents and visitors proximity to GA-400, allowing for easy commute times. The median listing price for a home in Cumming is $455,000, while the median household income is $47,273.
