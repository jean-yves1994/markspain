College Park Team


Interested in buying or selling a home in College Park, GA? See our College Park listings below! Founded as Atlantic City in 1890 as a depot, the city went through rapid changes once it became home to the Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport. Home to about 14,500 people, College Park is an 11.7 square mile community located south of Atlanta.

Despite its long history of growth, College Park continues to impress visitors and residents alike with its plethora of activities, restaurants, and other things to do in the area. 

As the home of the Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta Airport, many are attracted to College Park because it is a gateway to the world. Neighboring the popular neighborhoods of Hapeville, East Point, and Union City, College Park offers easy access to other urban centers as well as I-85 and I-285. The median household income in College Park is $35,470, while the median listing price is $215,000.

The College Park real estate market is among the more competitive in areas south of Atlanta. With many homes for sale, however, homebuyers have a plethora of real estate options to choose from. College Park contains several charming townhomes and single-family homes, as well as spacious lots. 

Buy a Home In College Park

Looking to buy a home in College Park? It’s best to be a proactive buyer. College Park homes typically do not last on the market without receiving an offer. There are several reasons why College Park is such a competitive neighborhood for real estate, but a notable one is the amenities and recreational activities available at College Park. From historical districts to outdoor activities, College Park has fun endeavors for families and singles alike. Some of the benefits of living in College Park include:

  • Quick access to the Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport
  • Gateway Center Arena
  • Downtown College Park
  • Historic College Park Golf Course
  • Phoenix Trail
