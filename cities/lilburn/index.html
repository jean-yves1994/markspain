Looking to buy or sell a house in Lilburn, GA? See our Lilburn listings below! Incorporated as a part of the Atlanta metropolitan area in 1910, Lilburn has a long history of being a prosperous community. While it was incorporated in 1910, it was originally founded in 1890 by Seaboard Air Line Railways. Known for its many activities, many are attracted to Lilburn because of its iconic shopping and dining district in Old Towne. Currently, 12,644 residents call Lilburn home.

Neighboring iconic neighborhoods such as Tucker, Stone Mountain, and Snellville, Lilburn offers access to major suburban centers in the Atlanta metropolitan area. Currently, the average household income in Lilburn is 58,158, and the average listing for a home for sale in Lilburn is 330,000.
