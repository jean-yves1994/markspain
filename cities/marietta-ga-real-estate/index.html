Marietta Team


Looking to buy or sell a home in Marietta, GA? With some of the most affluent homes in all of Georgia, it’s no surprise that this market is as popular as it is. The city of Marietta has a population of just over 60,000 people, and remains one of the largest and most desirable Atlanta suburbs for many reasons. Declared one of the best places in the country to retire, according to CNNMoney.com, Marietta is home to a variety of country clubs, leisure activities, premium restaurants, parks, trails, and more. Whether you’re selling your home in Marietta or looking to buy, you shouldn’t have any difficulty achieving your real estate goals, especially working with David Marshall Team.

The majority of Marietta homes can be found in lavish gated communities or on large spacious lots. Many Marietta homes are two-story houses with stunning brick facades and detailing, while others offer sprawling back porches and lawns that allow for year-round outdoor entertainment. The city of Marietta offers easy access to Buckhead, Atlanta, and the Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport. Located to the southwest of Alpharetta and west of Sandy Springs and Roswell, Marietta is a 24-square mile community that is home to the Gone With The Wind Museum, Marietta Square, the Marietta Museum of History and many other attractions. Marietta residents have a median age of 35 and a median household income of $57,452. The average value of Marietta property is $287,600.

Marietta real estate listings include a wide variety of property types, including single-family residences, townhomes, gated communities, empty land, new developments, commercial lots, and more. Buy a Home In Marietta

Marietta real estate attracts buyers from all over for several reasons. As the largest city in Cobb County, Marietta is the fourth largest of the cities by population in the Atlanta area. Marietta is popularly known for its massive employers, including these two industry giants: the Dobbins Air Reserve Base and the Lockheed Martin Manufacturing Plant. Marietta is also home to the headquarters Kool Smiles, Online Training Academy and more. Additionally, Marietta offers the following top employers:

  • YKK
  • WellStar Kennestone hospital
  • Alere
  • Columbian Chemicals
  • C.W. Matthews Contracting Co
  • Tip Top Poultry
  • Marietta City Schools
  • Cobb County School District
  • U.S. Security Association

Marietta is also home to several top-notch school districts, served by both Marietta City Schools and the Cobb County School District. These include Marietta High School, Marietta Middle School, Marietta Sixth Grade Academy, Hickory Hills Elementary, Lockheed Elementary, Park Street Elementary and many more. Additionally, Marietta is known for its rich culture and several different historic districts, which prove appealing for all sorts of home buyers. Some of the cultural attractions and amenities in Marietta include:

  • Historic Train Depot
  • Marietta Square
  • Downtown Town Square
  • County Courthouse
  • The Marietta Players
  • The Strand Theatre
  • The Marietta Museum
  • The Marietta Gone With the Wind Museum
  • The Big Chicken
