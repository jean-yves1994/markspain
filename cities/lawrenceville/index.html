Lawrenceville Team


Are you looking to buy or sell a home in Lawrenceville? You’re in great company. As one of the most sought-after suburbs of Atlanta, Lawrenceville offers residents a wide range of activities, great school districts, amazing restaurants, and a real estate market that is hard to beat. Located in Gwinnett County, Lawrenceville is home to 30,824 residents. Neighboring the popular neighborhoods of Stone Mountain, Johns Creek, and Lilburn, Lawrenceville is popular for its attractions, restaurants, and history.

Lawrenceville is a suburb that offers convenient proximity to Atlanta and the Hartsfield-Jackson Airport. Lawrenceville’s history is rich. Being the second oldest city in the Atlanta metropolitan area, it was named after Captain James Lawrence, a naval commander who fought in the War of 1812. The majority of Lawrenceville residents are between the ages of 25 and 34, making the median age of a resident 36.6. The average household income in Lawrenceville is around $60,000.
