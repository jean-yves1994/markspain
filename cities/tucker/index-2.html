Tucker Team


Are you looking to buy or sell a home in Tucker, Georgia? See Tucker listings below! Located in the heart of DeKalb County, Tucker has been one of the most popular suburbs near Atlanta in recent years. This community is ripe with the best of Southern hospitality, fun activities, great shopping experiences, and outdoor events. Tucker offers residents and potential buyers a wide range of property types such as charming single family homes, townhomes, condos, and empty lots. Located near the popular neighborhoods of Stone Mountain, Lilburn, and Brookhaven, Tucker is constantly viewed as one of the highlights of visiting Dekalb County.

Currently, there are 36,857 people that call Tucker their home. This 20 square-mile community is only 15 miles away from Atlanta, offering residents easy access to I-285, I-85, and the Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport. The average household income in Tucker is $60,536, and the median listing price for a property is $306,700.
