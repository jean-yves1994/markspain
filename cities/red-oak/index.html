Red Oak Team


Looking to buy or sell a house in Red Oak, GA? See our Red Oak listings below! Located in Fulton County, Red Oak is nestled towards the south of Atlanta’s metropolitan area. With history dating back to 1864, Red Oak is an unincorporated community that is away from the hustle and bustle of the city, while maintaining close access to the hottest Georgia attractions. Red Oak offers residents a plethora of fun family activities such as good local eateries, dun districts, and shops.

Neighboring College Park, East Point, and Heritage Park, Red Oak offers easy access to I-285 and I-85, and the downtown area of Atlanta.As one of the most popular places in the Atlanta area, the population of Red Oak keeps growing due to its many attractions. One of the major perks residents enjoy about Red Oak is the fact that they are less than 5 miles away from the Atlanta Heartsfield-Jackson International Airport.
