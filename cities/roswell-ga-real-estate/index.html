Roswell Team


Are you looking to buy or sell a home in Roswell, Georgia? See Roswell listings below! Roswell offers a variety of residences and real estate opportunities within its 16 different neighborhoods. With various styles of houses, Roswell offers a little something for everyone, including simple single-family residences, traditional colonial style homes, new condos and townhomes, commercial lots, and even land for sale. Located in northern Fulton County, just southwest of Alpharetta, north of Sandy Springs, and east of Woodstock, Roswell has a booming population of almost 100,000 residents.

Roswell, Georgia is the state’s eight-largest city, and is home to a rich historic district and lively downtown, which makes it one of Atlanta’s most desirable suburbs. A small, southern town, Roswell offers history, outdoor recreation, arts and culture, live music, stellar restaurants, and plenty of enticing family activities and opportunities. This 42 square-mile city is bordered to the north by Milton, and includes several creeks and rivers, such as Big Creek, the Chattahoochee River, Morgan Falls Dam, Johns Creek, Crooked Creek, Audery Mill Creek, and more. Roswell is known for its sense of safety and security, as well as for its vibrant nightlife and community.
