Clarkston Team


Looking to buy or sell a home in Clarkston? See our Clarkston listings below! This city is located in Dekalb County and is home to 12,750 people. With a history dating back to 1876, the town of Clarkston has seen outstanding growth since its humble beginnings. As a diverse place in Georgia, Clarkston prides itself on being “the Ellis Island of the South,” where many people of different backgrounds live in harmony and enjoy all Clarkston has to offer. From gorgeous scenery to delicious food options, Clarkston provides visitors and residents a unique small-town experience. 

Neighboring great communities such as Tucker, Scottdale, and Avondale Estates, Clarkston offers residents proximity to the hottest suburban centers near Atlanta while staying comfortably at a distance from the hustle and bustle of the cities. The median listing price for a home in Clarkston is $270,500, while the average household income is $41,070.
